About Us

We've been making healthy food for 10 years

RS Restaurant ki shuruaat ek chhote se sapne se hui — “har kisi ko sasta, swadisht aur swachh khana mile.” Aaj hum local ingredients aur traditional recipes ko modern touch ke saath serve karte hain.

Har dish humare chef ke junoon aur pyar se taiyaar hoti hai. Humara vaada hai — saaf, surakshit aur lazeez khana, jisse aapka har bite yaadgar ban jaaye.

Ghar jaisa swad, modern ambience aur friendly service — yahi hai RS Restaurant ka asli flavour.
